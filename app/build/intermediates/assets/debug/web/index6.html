SHIVA STATUE

Location :

Brahma statue is located at main chamber of the Brahma temple. Go upstairs from west the just go straight enter the main chamber.

Descriptions :

  • Brahma is depicted as 4 faces God. He is the creator God and holy seed of the universe according to Hindu mythology.

  • Brahma in Sanskrit means evolution or growth.

  • Brahma was born as the holy seed of the universe. Bright like milions lights the source of the universe.

  • Brahma is incarnation from Brahman, and incarnation of Brahma is called Brahmana. Brahma give order to other angels.

  • As holy seed of the universe, Brahma has 4 faces.

  • Front face means symbol of eternity, Right face means Yadnya ceremony, left face means long lasting, and the back fac¬e is symbol of knowledge.
