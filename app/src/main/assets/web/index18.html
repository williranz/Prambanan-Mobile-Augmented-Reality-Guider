PRAMBANAN OPENING BOARD BY INDONESIAN SECOND PRESIDENT

Location :

This board is located on the entrance area before entering temple compound. From domestic or international entrance gate turn rignt and turn left, the board is on the left side.

Descriptions :

  • This boarding containing information about the official opening of Prambanan museum.

  • This board is signed by Indonesian second President, General Soeharto.

  • Since Prambanan rediscovery from 1880s until today Prambanan has 6 times restoration phase.

  • Prambanan is officially opened to local visitor in 6 July 1989.

  • After rediscovery, the governments of Indonesia plan to make Prambanan as heritage site and dedicated to restore and rebuild at least 75% of their original masonry.

  • By collaborating with archeologist all around the world Prambanan restoration project has successfully opened as heritage site museum within 70 years.
