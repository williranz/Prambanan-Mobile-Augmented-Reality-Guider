RELIEF DEER KALPATARU

Location :

Kalpataru deer relief is located at the western side panel of the Shiva temple.

Descriptions :

  • Kalpataru derives from Sanskrit means ‘Tree of Life’ while deer is a symbol of loyalty.

  • Relief deer kalpataru means the loyalty of human to serve for the life which means that the God (Brahma) creating the world with Tree as a foundation and loyalty as a value of life.

  • Deer kalpataru historically is used to depict the human’s loyalty toward their kingdom.

  • Human should keep their kingdom to glorify dignity of the Lord.

  • Brahma created kalpataru depicted as tree to make human should live along side with nature peacefully.

  • Human should keep nature preserved within it. Besides kalpataru, there are mutual values that should human have. One of them is the loyalty which depicted as deer in Hindu mythology.
