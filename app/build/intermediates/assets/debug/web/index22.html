RELIEF LION PANEL

Location :

Lion panel is located at the western side panel of the Shiva temple.

Descriptions :

  • The statue of lion is enclosed on either side of Kalpataru tree.

  • This lion is called kinara kinari.

  • Lion panel is used to symbolize the power of Rama in beating the giants that disturb the Wismamitra, the award of this is a beautiful princess named Shinta.

  • Rama is not the only one looking this award but some other warriors also join causing this become a tight competition. Rama is one that become the lion as a winner of this competition and become the husband of Shinta.

  • Lion panel is used to depict the story of Rama who is known as the incarnation of Wisni to kill giants and join the competition in which winner will be awarded a beautiful princess named Shinta.

  • The prowess of Rama in winning the competition make him becomes the lion, symbolizing the power of Wisni.
