RELIEF FLOWERS KALPATARU

Location :

Kalpataru flowers relief is located at the southern side panel of the Garuda temple.

Descriptions :

  • Kalpataru derives from Sanskrit means ‘Tree of Life’ while flowers is a symbol of growth.

  • Relief kalpataru flowers means the human will grow to be come mature during the lifetime which means that the God (Brahma) creating the world with Tree as a foundation, growth as a value of life.

  • Kalpataru flowers historically are used to depict the growth of human from childhood, adolescence, until maturity.

  • During human live in the earth, human will grow along side with the nature.

  • Brahma created kalpataru depicted as tree to make human should live along side with nature peacefully.

  • Human should keep nature preserved within it. Besides kalpataru, there are mutual values that should human have. One of them is the growth which depicted as flowers in Hindu mythology.
