RELIEF THREE DEVATAS

Location :

This relief is located at the inner balustrade of Angsa temple. Go upstairs from the east of Angsa temple. At the second floor, go left, the relief is on the balustrade.

Descriptions :

  • In Prambanan there are 24 statues of dewa Lokapalas, the God of four cardinal points.

  • Lokapalas is angels to guarding the 24 key gates during funeral.

  • Funeral in symbolizing the death in Hindu mythology.

  • During funeral Lord Durga is widely worshipped because he is the one that guide the relieved soul to nirvana and the Lokapalas are the gate openers for the nirvana.

  • There are no much story of Dewa Lokapalas, but this relief symbolize three Lokapalas as close brotherhood.

  • It’s believed that Lokapalas holding 24 gate keys during Hindu funeral.
