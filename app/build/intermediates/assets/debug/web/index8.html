RELIEF RELAXING DEVATA

Location :

This relief is located at the inner balustrade of Angsa temple. Go upstairs from the east of Angsa temple. At the second floor, go left, the relief is on the balustrade.

Descriptions :

  • This relief telling the story when Hanoman coming to the country for assistance to save the hermitage of Wismamistra for the attack of giant.

  • This Devata was sitting behind Shinta while shinta was talking with hanoman.

  • After hearing conversation between Shinta and Hanoman.

  • This Devata go to meet Brahmana and tell him what shinta plans to do with Rama.

  • Hanoman come to the country for release assistance of Wismamistra from savage of the giant.

  • He then met shinta. This Devata witnessed the confidential conversation between Shinta and Hanoman.
