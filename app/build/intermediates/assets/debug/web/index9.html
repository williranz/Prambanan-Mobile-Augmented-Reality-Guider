BRAHMIN AND TWO BRAHMANAS STATUES

Location :

This relief is located at the inner balustrade of Brahma temple. Go upstairs from the west of Brahma temple. At the second floor, go left, go right, the relief is on the balustrade.

Descriptions :

  • Brahmin or also called Brahman was born as the holy seed of the universe and incarnate to Brahma.

  • Brahmin is depicted as old grandfather and the Lord teacher with the long beard.

  • During his incarnation to Brahma, Brahmin or Brahman is symbolizing the core emergence of universe.

  • Brahmin is become an ornament at the outer wall of Prambanan meditating with two brahmanas standing on either side.

  • During the creation of the universe Brahmin as the great source of the universe turn into Brahman and go to the earth to create the life and green pasture of the Padmana wheels.

  • It’s called the emergence of the country with blessed prophecy by the universe.
