RELIEF TWO DEVATAS

Location :

This relief is located at the inner balustrade of Vishnu temple. Go upstairs from the west of Vishnu temple. At the second floor, go left, the relief is on the balustrade.

Descriptions :

  • This relief showing the two Devatas as one of the heavenly creature living in Vishnu temple.

  • Devata is an angel whose role is to deliver message to Wismamitra from lord Vishnu, Shiva, and Brahma.

  • Devata was one telling to Wismamitra that a lot of giants would attack wismamitra during his hermitage.

  • Awaking of that Wismamitra tell all warriors to save him from the attack giants by awarding his most beautiful woman named Shinta to any warrior that saves him.

  • There is no much story about the Devata and they also not worshipped.

  • They are the ears of 3 holiest God in Hindu and work as messenger from them to the Wismamitra.
