RELIEF RAMA MARRYING SHINTA

Location :

This relief is located at the inner balustrade of Angsa temple. Go upstairs from the east of Angsa temple. At the second floor, go left, the relief is on the balustrade.

Descriptions :

  • This relief showing the story of Rama making Shinta as his wife.

  • Wismamitra promises the victor of the competition who can kill giant that disturb Wismamitra will be awarded a beautiful princess named Shinta. Rama wins and makes Shinta his wife as promised by Wismamitra.

  • The marriage of Rama and Shinta is held on Brahmin altar celebrated by thousands of angels and heavenly creatures.

  • They blessed by one son named Bharata.

  • After Rama defeats the giant, as the promised, Wismamitra awards him a beautiful princess called Shinta, He then meet Shinta the beautiful princess and marry her.

  • They become the couple, Rama become the king and shinta become the queen as his wife.
